Why students use ScholarLens

Instead of searching scattered portals and deadlines manually, students can sign in once, maintain a profile, and access a guided dashboard that surfaces relevant scholarships, recommendations, applications, and notifications.

What happens after login

Your dashboard becomes the main workspace: browse scholarship cards, review deadlines, explore programs, apply, and monitor updates from one place without losing context.
