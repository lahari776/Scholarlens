About This Scholarship

The STEM Excellence Scholarship is awarded annually to outstanding engineering students who demonstrate academic brilliance and a passion for innovation. It covers tuition, living expenses, and mentorship access.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Enrolled in B.Tech / B.E. (any branch)
  • Minimum CGPA of 8.0
  • Family income below ₹8 LPA
  • Indian citizen
  • No other active scholarship

Documents Required

  • Latest marksheet / transcript
